What is SPI ?

SPI was built with the purpose of improving oneself as an individual and as a team. This is a platform where we muster all the requirements of guidance, resources, sharing of knowledge etc., whatever it takes to harness the capabilities of a team in the best way possible. We bring out the projects collectively working on them. Our motto is clear:

"WE THINK, SHARE AND DEVELOPE!!"
